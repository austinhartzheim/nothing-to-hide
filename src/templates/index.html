A common assumption when considering invasive surveillance is that those with nothing to hide have nothing to fear. This website is to show you just how close to that action you actually are. In the style of "Six Degrees to Separation," we have collected several "degrees" worth of twitter followers from various twitter accounts on the front lines of political subversiveness; accounts you can safely assume are being watched.
